SheHeroes - an app for Women and Child safety.

About

Considering the safety of woman in India in recent times. We wanted to give a try from our end to address the issue in a simpler and safer way.

Looking at the recent trends and the most powerfull weapon with the humanity-technology, we planned to use the same to give access to women in serious or dangerous situations to address the issue in a fast and easier way to ensure their Security.
